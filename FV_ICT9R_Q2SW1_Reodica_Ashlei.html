Main Characters from my favorite childhood cartoon

Characters
Daddy Pig Based on his name, Daddy Pig it is obvious that he is the father of Peppa Pig and George. Daddy Pig is a jolly guy who always makes them laugh, and he always watch tv all the time.
Mommy pig Mommy Pig is the mother of Peppa and George. She is the one who takes care of Peppa Pig and George, and the one who always do the things at home.
Peppa Pig Peppa Pig is the main character. Peppa Pig is the oldest daughther of Daddy Pig and Mommy Pig. Peppa is jolly as well as her dad, but she bonds with her father more.
George George is the youngest son of Daddy Pig and Mommy Pig. George is a kid who loves Dinosaurs, and he loves to go at the playground.
